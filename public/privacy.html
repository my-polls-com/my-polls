This Privacy Policy explains how Livepoll, Inc. ("Livepoll," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information about you when you access or use the Livepoll website located at my-polls.com, our mobile applications, and related products and services (collectively, the "Service").

1. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We may collect information you provide, including: email address; account registration information; year of birth, where collected; poll responses and submissions; customer support communications; verification-related information.

Payment Information

Payments are processed through third-party providers including Stripe. We do not store full payment card numbers.

Automatically Collected Information

We may collect: IP address; device identifiers; browser information; operating system; usage activity; general geographic location derived from IP address or billing ZIP code.

Cookies and Similar Technologies

We may use cookies and similar technologies to authenticate users, improve functionality, analyze usage, detect fraud, and protect the integrity of the Service.

Depending on use of the Service, we may collect identifiers, customer records information, payment-related information, demographic information, commercial information, network activity information, geolocation information, and user-generated content.

3. How We Use Information

We may use information to: provide and improve the Service; manage accounts and subscriptions; verify eligibility and account authenticity; prevent duplicate accounts and fraud; produce aggregated and de-identified polling insights; conduct internal research and analytics; enforce our Terms of Service; comply with legal obligations.

We may aggregate, anonymize, de-identify, analyze, and commercialize aggregated and de-identified information.

We may disclose information to payment processors, hosting providers, cloud infrastructure providers, analytics providers, security providers, customer support vendors, and professional advisors.

Legal Compliance and Protection

9. Children's Privacy

The Service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
